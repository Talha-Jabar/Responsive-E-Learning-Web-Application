What are the benefits of gamification in eLearning?

Increased productivity (89%) and happiness (88%) at work, according to responses in a TalentLMS gamification survey. A sense of achievement, by winning badges or leveling up.

What's best? Self-paced Learning or Intructor-led Training.

Instructor-led training has the advantage of being immediate and interactive. Employees can ask SMEs questions in real-time and the answer will benefit the whole group, not just the person who asked the question.

How can we tell whether an eLearning program is successful?

This is probably one of the toughest eLearning FAQs to answer. The truth is, “success” will look different for every company, as it depends on what your goals were, to begin with.

How can we engage learners during online courses?

Although there are many benefits to eLearning when compared to face-to-face training, keeping engagement levels consistently up is not easy. Most people nowadays have an average attention span of less than 10 seconds — and we are all used to getting distracted or multi-tasking when we are online.
